About Eventa

We're on a mission to connect people with unforgettable experiences through seamless event discovery and booking.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Eventa started with a simple idea: events should be easy to find and joyful to attend.

Our team of event enthusiasts and tech innovators built a platform that puts you — the attendee — at the heart of everything. From concerts and sports to workshops and conferences, we make discovering and booking events effortless.

Today, Eventa powers millions of experiences across the globe and continues to grow with one goal: helping you live more, worry less.
