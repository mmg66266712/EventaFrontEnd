Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 01, 2025

At Eventa, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password (encrypted), profile photo
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via third-party providers (we do not store card details)
  • Usage Data: Events viewed, tickets purchased, device info, IP address
  • Communications: Support messages, feedback

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process ticket purchases and send confirmations
  • To send important updates about your bookings
  • To personalize your experience
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security

3. Data Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:

  • With event organizers (name & email for ticket delivery)
  • With payment processors
  • When required by law
  • With trusted service providers (hosting, analytics)

4. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request data export
  • Delete your account

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies via browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Eventa is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notice.

10. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@eventa.com
